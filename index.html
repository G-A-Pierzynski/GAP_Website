Hi! I'm Gabriel, an incoming PhD student in Economic Geography & Data Science at the London School of Economics and Political Science (LSE), starting in September. My doctoral research, advised by Neil Lee and Vassilis Monastiriotis, investigates the economics of digital technology diffusion - why digital technologies cluster spatially, and the role of human capital, competition, and industrial policy in how they spread. Outside of my PhD commitments, I also do research in IR Theory and Philosophy of Social Science.

I hold an MSc in European and International Public Policy (Political Economy) from LSE (2024) and a BSc in Philosophy and Politics from the University of Bristol (2023).

I have been published in the Journal for the Theory of Social Behaviour on the causes of the Ukrainian war (with a reply to my article by Heikki Patomäki), as well as the Frontiers in Political Science journal. In my free time I sail the Baltic as a watch officer on tall ships.
